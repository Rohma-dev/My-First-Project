Timeline of an Iconic Leader

  • December 25, 1876

    Born in Karachi, Pakistan (then British India). He showed immense potential and dedication toward education from his early days.

  • 1896

    Qualified as a barrister from Lincoln's Inn in London at a very young age, becoming a successful and respected lawyer.

  • 1913

    Joined the All-India Muslim League, dedicating his sharp political mind to advocating for the rights of Muslims in the subcontinent.

  • March 23, 1940

    Presided over the Lahore Resolution, where the official demand for a separate homeland, Pakistan, was formally made.

  • August 14, 1947

    Successfully led the nation to independence, becoming the first Governor-General of Pakistan through sheer political brilliance and resolve.
